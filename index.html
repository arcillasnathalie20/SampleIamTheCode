Our SDG Project

Our project focuses on SDG #4: Quality Education. We aim to promote equal learning opportunities through coding literacy, empowering students to use technology for a better and sustainable future.

We believe that through coding, we can enhance creativity, critical thinking, and collaboration among young learners to prepare them for the digital age.
